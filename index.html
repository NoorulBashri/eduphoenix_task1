AGRICULTURE

Agriculture is the Backbone of India

Agriculture is the foundation of our food supply and involves cultivating land, raising crops, and rearing animals for various purposes. It encompasses a range of practices, from traditional methods to advanced technologies, aimed at producing food, fibers, and raw materials for diverse industries. Farmers utilize techniques like irrigation, crop rotation, and livestock management to optimize yields while considering environmental sustainability. Agriculture is vital for global food security, economic stability, and rural development, playing a crucial role in sustaining populations and supporting livelihoods worldwide.
